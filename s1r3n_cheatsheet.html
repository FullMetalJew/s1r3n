How to play S1-R3N — the simple version

S1-R3N is a precision striker and intelligence asset. She doesn't get in melee — she finds high ground, marks a target, and eliminates it before it knows she's there. As a droid she feels no fear, no poison, no disease. She is the definition of a calculated threat.

Every turn revolves around one question: is my quarry marked? Ranger's Quarry adds bonus damage every turn — keep it active. Deadeye Superiority lets her spend superiority dice to punish misses and boost crits. Her INT of +3 drives both her Tech powers and her massive skill bonus stack.

// COMBAT PROTOCOL
Mark your quarry (reaction, free). Stay at range — 30-60ft ideal. Attack with the Blaster Rifle every turn. Use Sharpshooter's Routine on bonus action for extra pressure. Spend Deadeye Superiority dice to punish key moments. Tech powers are utility — save them for the right situation.

Watch out for Ion damage — it hits her twice as hard as anyone else. Stay out of melee, avoid Ion grenades and Ion weapons. If you're getting rushed, use Rapid Reconstruction as an action to spend a Hit Die and patch yourself up mid-fight. You don't need a healer — you are partially self-sufficient.

Tech Points fuel your powers — 6 total, refreshing on short rest. Unlike Force Points they come back fast. Spend them more freely than a Force caster would, but don't blow them all before a boss fight.

Key Class Features
Reaction Ranger's Quarry
Mark a target — your bullets hit harder
Once per turn as a reaction: choose a creature you can see. Until you use this again or the target dies, add 1d6 bonus damage once per turn when you hit it with a weapon attack. Also grants advantage on Perception and Survival to track the quarry.
Keep this active every single fight — it's free and adds up fast
Special Deadeye Superiority — 1/2 per Short Rest
Superiority dice that punish and reward precision
Mark of the Deadeye: When you score a critical hit or reduce a target to 0 HP, regain 1 superiority die.
Sharpshooter's Routine (BA): Expend 1 superiority die — make one ranged weapon attack as a bonus action. Add the die to the damage roll on a hit.
Use Sharpshooter's Routine when quarry is marked for massive single-turn burst
Bonus Action Sharpshooter Style
Ignore cover, ignore range penalties
Your ranged weapon attacks ignore half and three-quarters cover. You do not suffer disadvantage for long-range attacks. When you score a critical hit with a ranged weapon, you can roll one additional damage die.
Never worry about cover or distance — shoot from anywhere
Action Rapid Reconstruction
Built-in self-repair — no medpac needed
As an action, spend one Hit Die to recover HP. Roll the die and add your CON modifier. You regain that many HP. Can be used mid-combat to stay in the fight without relying on allies.
Use at 30-40% HP — don't wait until you're critical
Passive Pathfinder
The jungle of Rakata Prime is your ally
You ignore difficult terrain caused by natural environments. You can move through difficult terrain without spending extra movement. You also have advantage on checks to navigate or track in wilderness environments.
On Rakata Prime this is huge — dense jungle slows everyone else, not you
Techcasting — DC 13 · Attack +5 · INT +3 · Refreshes on Short Rest
Target Lock
Innate Bonus Action 90ft 1 creature 1d6
Mark a target with a targeting lock. Deals 1d6 bonus damage and grants tracking data. Pairs perfectly with Ranger's Quarry — stack both on the same target for maximum single-target pressure.
Encrypted Message
At-Will Action 120ft No TP cost
Send a secure, undetectable message to any creature within 120ft. They can reply in kind. Invaluable for silent coordination mid-combat or covert communication during infiltration on Rakata Prime.
Combustive Shot
At-Will Action No TP cost
Enhance a blaster bolt to deal fire or explosive damage on hit. At-will — no resource cost. Use when you want to change damage type to bypass resistances or trigger environmental hazards.
Mending
At-Will 1 minute Touch No TP cost
Repair a single break or tear in an object or creature. Restores minor physical damage. Out-of-combat utility — patch gear, fix droids, seal breaches. Also works on yourself between encounters.
Tracer Bolt
1st Level Action 120ft Atk -1 4d6 1 TP
Fire a high-powered tracer round dealing 4d6 damage. Attack roll at -1 penalty. High damage ceiling — use on your marked quarry for a devastating burst turn. Best combined with Sharpshooter's Routine.
Distracting Blow
Mental Superiority Melee
A superiority-fueled strike that distracts the target, giving allies advantage on their next attack roll against it. Use to set up a teammate's big hit rather than dealing damage yourself.
Precision Attack
General Superiority
Expend a superiority die to add it to an attack roll before or after the roll. Use when you really need a hit to land — save it for Tracer Bolt or your quarry-marked attack to guarantee damage on a key turn.
